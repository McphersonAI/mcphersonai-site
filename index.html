Why This Exists

Most operators find out too late.

01
The P&L comes too late to act on.
By the time the monthly numbers hit, the hours are worked, the food is wasted, and the only option is "do better next week." The problem isn't the data — it's the timing.
02
Generic software doesn't think like an operator.
Most tools give you a dashboard. They show you what happened. A real operator tells you what it means, what's causing it, and what to do about it before the week ends.
03
Managers are stretched thin and information is scattered.
A shift lead shouldn't need to manually calculate labor drift while running a lunch rush. The system should catch it and surface it at the right moment — not buried in a report.
